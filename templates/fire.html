03 : #Fire

Welcome to Fire Tweets

On Earth, something is always burning. Wildfires are started by lightning or accidentally by people. Fires can generate large amounts of smoke pollution, release greenhouse gases, and unintentionally degrade ecosystems. But fires can also clear away dead and dying underbrush which can help restore an ecosystem.
